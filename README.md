# Awesome Manifestation Skills [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of tools, frameworks, research, and **agent skills** for building a grounded manifestation practice — the kind that keeps the real mechanisms (mental rehearsal, focused attention, deliberate emotional state) and drops the cosmology. Plus the systems that turn a vision into something shipped: self-made music for state, generative AI for rendering, and the Reality Architect loop.

This list takes an explorer-builder stance, not a guru one. Where a claim is metaphysical, it's labelled as such. The aim is a practice you can run and measure — not one you have to believe in.

## Agent Skills in This Repo

Real, installable [Agent Skills](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/equipping-agents-for-the-real-world-with-agent-skills) (`SKILL.md` format — works in Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, and compatible runners). Each one is a grounded practice your agent can run with you.

- **[`manifestation-session`](skills/manifestation-session/SKILL.md)** — Run a focused imagination + felt-state session toward one specific outcome, ending in a concrete next action.
- **[`vibe-align`](skills/vibe-align/SKILL.md)** — Turn a goal-state into a music prompt (tempo, mode, lyric, frequency) using the Vibe OS model, so you set state on purpose.
- **[`intention-spec`](skills/intention-spec/SKILL.md)** — Convert a vague vision into a definiteness-of-purpose spec: outcome, who it's for, what "done" looks like.
- **[`reality-architect-loop`](skills/reality-architect-loop/SKILL.md)** — Walk one full See → Design → Build → Automate → Compound loop applied to a personal goal.
- **[`evidence-log`](skills/evidence-log/SKILL.md)** — Keep an honest log of what moves toward the vision, separating what you made happen from what surfaced on its own.

## Music & State Alignment

Tools for engineering the emotional state a practice depends on — the grounded reading of "raise your vibration."

- **[Vibe OS](https://github.com/frankxai/vibe-os)** — Open, research-backed system mapping a goal-state to tempo, mode, instrument, lyric, and frequency. The state engine behind this list. `Python`
- **[Suno](https://suno.com)** — Text-to-music generation; make your own track for an exact state in minutes.
- **[Udio](https://www.udio.com)** — Studio-quality AI music generation with fine style control; good for longer, editable state tracks.
- **[Brain.fm](https://brain.fm)** — Functional music engineered for focus, relaxation, and sleep, with published research.
- **[Endel](https://endel.io)** — Adaptive soundscapes that respond to time of day, heart rate, and activity for state regulation.
- **[ISO principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iso_principle)** — Music-therapy technique: match your current mood, then shift tempo/mode to guide it. The grounded basis for state-change-with-music.

## Imagination & Mental Rehearsal

The mechanism with the strongest evidence: vivid imagined practice changes the brain and behaviour.

- **[Motor imagery (overview)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motor_imagery)** — The cognitive rehearsal of action; decades of use in elite sport and rehabilitation.
- **[Mental contrasting / WOOP](https://woopmylife.org/)** — Gabriele Oettingen's evidence-based method: pair a wish with an honest look at the obstacle. Counters the trap of outcome-only fantasy.
- **[Imagery in sport (AASP)](https://appliedsportpsych.org/resources/resources-for-athletes/imagery/)** — Applied sport-psychology guidance on imagery that actually transfers to performance.

## Attention & Focus Systems

A specific intention biases what your brain surfaces. These help you hold and direct it.

- **[Obsidian](https://obsidian.md)** — Local-first notes; a durable home for your vision, spec, and evidence log.
- **[Reflect](https://reflect.app)** / **[Logseq](https://logseq.com)** — Networked note tools for daily intention + reflection.
- **[Second Brain OS](https://github.com/frankxai/second-brain-os)** — A memory layer your agents can read, so your intention persists across sessions.
- **[Sunsama](https://sunsama.com)** / **[Akiflow](https://akiflow.com)** — Daily planners that turn an intention into the one action that moves it.

## Goal & Intention Frameworks

Make the vision specific. Specificity is load-bearing.

- **[WOOP](https://woopmylife.org/)** — Wish, Outcome, Obstacle, Plan. Research-backed goal method from NYU.
- **[Implementation intentions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Implementation_intention)** — "If situation X, then I'll do Y." One of the most reliable behaviour-change findings in psychology.
- **[OKRs](https://www.whatmatters.com/faqs/okr-meaning-definition-example)** — Objective + measurable key results; a clean way to make a vision checkable.
- **[Think and Grow Rich](https://frankx.ai/think-and-grow-rich)** — Napoleon Hill's principles, read as a system (definiteness of purpose, auto-suggestion, the mastermind) rather than mysticism.
- **[The Secret, read honestly](https://frankx.ai/the-secret)** — What the Law of Attraction gets right, what to ignore, and the real mechanisms underneath.

## Research & Evidence

The honest evidence base — what holds up, with sources.

- **[Manifestation, Law of Attraction & AI Systems](https://frankx.ai/research/manifestation-law-of-attraction-ai-systems)** — A grounded synthesis separating mechanism from belief, with sources and explicit limitations.
- **Pascual-Leone et al. (1995)** — [Mental practice produces measurable cortical change](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/7500130/). The motor-imagery foundation.
- **Oettingen & Mayer (2002)** — [Positive fantasies about the future can *reduce* effort](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/12416921/). Why outcome-only "visualization" isn't enough.
- **Locke & Latham (2002)** — [Goal-setting theory](https://psycnet.apa.org/record/2002-15790-003): specific, challenging goals beat vague ones.
- **Juslin & Västfjäll (2008)** — [Mechanisms of musical emotion](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18926045/). How music actually moves state.
- **Karageorghis & Priest (2012)** — [Music in the exercise domain](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/23667771/): tempo, motivation, and performance.

## Honest Caveats

This list is deliberately careful about a genre that often isn't:

- The **neuroscience** (mental rehearsal, selective attention, affect → behaviour) is solid. The **"Law of Attraction"** as framed in popular books is largely unfalsifiable and lacks peer-reviewed support.
- **"Quantum"** framing is metaphor, not physics. Don't present it as science.
- Outcome-only fantasy can *reduce* effort ([Oettingen](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/12416921/)). What works is rehearsal **plus** state **plus** attention **plus** action — a loop that ends in something shipped.
- Reject any claim that people "attract" their own misfortune. It's unsupported and harmful.
